Limitations

  • Commonly, these schools may consist of a series of lectures with hand-outs or similar teaching approaches.
  • These have been shown to be ineffective compared to active learning methods.
  • Furthermore, for hands on workshop-style events there can be technical problems or large set up costs.



S. E. DiCarlo. Too much content, not enough thinking, and too little FUN! Adv Physiol Educ, 33(4), 257-264, 2009.

PaNOSC & PaN-Training


       

e-Learning Platform


What is it?

  • Open e-Learning platform built on Moodle.
  • Includes all Moodle functionality; wikis, forums, interactive quizzes, etc.
  • Access to a JupyterLab instance (based at ESS-DMSC).
  • Can be used in a MOOC fashion or to augment in-person teaching.
